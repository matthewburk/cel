listbox

(cel)

A listbox is a container cel with vertical and horizontal scrollbars.

A listbox is a container cel with vertical and horizontal scrollbars. A listbox is a scroll cel with the subject encapsulated. The subject is an list sequence, when a cel is linked to the listbox it is put in the list.
