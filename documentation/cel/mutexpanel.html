cel.mutexpanel

(module)

Factory for the mutexpanel metacel.

Factory for the mutexpanel metacel.

metacel

mutexpanel  A mutexpanel is a container cel that only hosts one link at a time, known as the subject.

A mutexpanel is a container cel that only hosts one link at a time, known as the subject. When a cel is linked to the mutexpanel it is redirected to a bucket. The mutexpanel:cels() function exposes the cel linked to the mutexpanel and the cels linked to the bucket.
